# Hyena Clan
# Organization Overview  
Hyena Clan was established in August 2020 by two young men in Alexandra Township. The name “hyena clan” came from the word hyena. According to them the word “hyena” is a title given to a hard worker or a person who provides for themselves and the people around them. 
Vision: Is to remain constant in the clothing industry and ensuring that we deliver good quality products to our customers.  
Mission: Is to be a long running clothing brand and to become one of the most known clothing brands in the world. 
Target Audience: Young Adults
