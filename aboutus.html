About Us

Hyena Clan was established in August 2020 in Alexandra Township. The name ‘hyena’ is used as a title for people who work hard, stay united, and look after their community.

Hyena Clan started as a small group dedicated to uplifting the youth and empowering the community through creativity and teamwork. Over time, it grew into a local movement.

Our vision is to create opportunities, build young leaders, and inspire innovation inside Alexandra Township and beyond.

Our mission is to support local talent, encourage entrepreneurship, and provide real-life skills that help community members grow.
